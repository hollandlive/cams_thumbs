## YouTube Video Thumbnail Gallery

A clean, modern web app that displays a grid of YouTube video thumbnails with full video details.

### Features

**1. Video Grid Gallery**

- Responsive grid layout similar to YouTube's homepage
- Cards displaying video thumbnails fetched directly from YouTube's image servers
- Each card shows: thumbnail, video title, channel name, and view count

**2. Interactive Video Cards**

- Hover effects for better user feedback
- Two click options per video:
  - Click thumbnail to open embedded YouTube player in a modal
  - "Watch on YouTube" button to open the video in a new tab

**3. Embedded Video Player Modal**

- Clean modal overlay with embedded YouTube player
- Close button to dismiss the modal
- Video title displayed in the modal header

**4. Sample Video Content**

- Pre-populated with a curated list of sample videos (5-6 videos)
- Easy-to-edit data structure so you can add your own videos with their metadata (ID, title, channel, views)

### Design Style

- Clean, modern interface with subtle shadows and rounded corners
- Dark mode support
- Responsive design that works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
